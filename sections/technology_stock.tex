\subsection{Technology Stock}
The technologies that shape residential energy demand extend beyond the heating
and cooling systems discussed in \Cref{sec:heating_cooling}. The same
electrification trend is visible in transport: Belgian households owned on average
about 1.06 cars in 2024, a figure stable since 2021 \cite{StatbelHouseholds2025},
within a passenger fleet that is steadily hybridising and electrifying
\cite{Statbel2025EV}. Together with rooftop photovoltaics and heat pumps, a
growing electric-vehicle fleet is a key boundary condition for residential demand
modelling, as it reshapes both the annual electricity balance and, through
charging behaviour, the timing of peak demand.
